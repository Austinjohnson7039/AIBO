What are your timings? We are open from 9 AM to 10 PM, seven days a week including public holidays.
Do you have eggless brownies? Yes, all our brownies are available in both regular and eggless variants.
Do you accept cards? Yes, we accept all major credit and debit cards, UPI, and cash payments.
Is there parking available? Yes, we have free parking for up to 30 minutes for dine-in customers.

What is the WiFi password? Free WiFi is available for dine-in customers. Ask the staff for the current password.

Can I customize my coffee? Absolutely. We offer customization on milk type, sugar level, and shot strength.
Do you have loyalty rewards? Yes, our loyalty card gives you a free beverage after every 10 purchases.
What is your return policy on packaged products? Unopened packaged items can be returned within 24 hours with the receipt.
Do you cater for events? Yes, we offer catering packages for corporate events and private parties. Contact us for pricing.
Is there seating outside? Yes, we have an outdoor terrace that seats up to 20 guests, weather permitting.
Do you sell coffee beans? Yes, we sell our house-blend beans in 250g and 500g packs at the counter.
Are allergen menus available? Yes, full allergen information is listed on our menu and available from staff on request.
